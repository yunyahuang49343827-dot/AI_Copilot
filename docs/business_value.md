# Business Value

The WITS Governance, Compliance & Financial Control Agentic Copilot turns static corporate policy PDFs into a workflow-oriented compliance assistant for portfolio demonstration purposes.

## Business Problems Addressed

- Corporate policies are long, fragmented, and difficult to search manually.
- Related workflows often span multiple policy families, such as insider trading plus material information, or asset transactions plus related-party rules.
- Users need cited evidence, not unsupported chatbot answers.
- Project reviewers need to see business-process understanding, not only model calls.

## Demo Value

The project shows how enterprise AI solution design can combine document engineering, retrieval, evidence handling, workflow design, and a practical UI into a local-first prototype.

## Limitations

The system is a portfolio demo and does not provide legal, financial, compliance, investment, or governance advice.
